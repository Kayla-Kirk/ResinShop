If you overwork it you become a cloud killer. There's nothing worse than a cloud killer. This is where you take out all your hostilities and frustrations. It's better than kicking the puppy dog around and all that so. You have to allow the paint to break to make it beautiful. Isn't it great to do something you can't fail at? I really believe that if you practice enough you could paint the 'Mona Lisa' with a two-inch brush. You create the dream - then you bring it into your world. Work that paint. I'll go over the colors one more time that we use: Titanium white, Thalo green, Prussian blue, Van Dyke brown, Alizarin crimson, Sap green, Cad yellow, and Permanent red. There he comes. I spend a lot of time walking around in the woods and talking to trees, and squirrels, and little rabbits and stuff.
